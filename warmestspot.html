Warmest Spot

FiFi has a strong love for warm spots, and her favorite hangout is the top of the water heater. She loves the cozy, heat-filled nook, often found relaxing there for hours. It's the perfect spot for her, and it’s no surprise—Devon Rex cats like to stay warm and cozy, always seeking out the sun or heat sources
