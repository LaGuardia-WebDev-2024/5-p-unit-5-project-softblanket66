Bath Time

Bath time isn’t FiFi’s favorite, but she’s surprisingly cooperative. She may not love the water, but she handles it like a champ. Afterward, she’s fine with the hair dryer, and by the time we towel her off, she’s already 50% dry. She grumbles a bit, but in the end, she’s always fresh and fabulous!
