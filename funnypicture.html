Funny Picture

Even the most beautiful cats have their off moments, and FiFi is no exception! There’s one photo we always laugh at—her "ugly" picture. Her fur is messy, eyes half-closed, and she looks pretty funny. But we love it because it shows her real side. Even when she’s not looking her best, she’s still our sweetest, most lovable cat!
